Obama Blamed for Katrina Response

Aug 22nd, 2013

According to a new PPP poll, more people in Louisiana blame Barack Obama for the slow response during Hurricane Katrina than they do George W. Bush. FYI – Obama was a freshman Senator at the time.

I’m no longer amazed at the ignorance or hatred that some Americans feel toward Obama, so this doesn’t surprise me. But how much of this is that and how much is – “I can’t believe you just asked me this dumb question?”

Survey Results

Who do you think was more responsible for the poor response to Hurricane Katrina: 
George W. Bush or Barack Obama?

George W. Bush ............................................. 28%   
Barack Obama................................................ 29%  
Not sure ................................................... 44%
Source: Public Policy Polling
