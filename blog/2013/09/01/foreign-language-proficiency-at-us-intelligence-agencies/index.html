Foreign Language Proficiency at US Intelligence Agencies

Sep 1st, 2013 | Comments

According to this article, US spy agencies employ fewer than 2000 people proficient in Middle Eastern languages.

Spanish speakers outnumber Arabic by a more than 2-to-1 margin. But I’m not surprised. We share a border with Mexico and immigration and the “War on Drugs” are just as important (maybe more so), as the “War on Terrorism”.

What surprises me is this — why do we have more French speakers than Russian or Korean?
